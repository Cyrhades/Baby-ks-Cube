You can turn a face by 90째 clockwise by clicking on a left panel button or by pressing the corresponding key. You can turn a face by 90째 counterclockwise by clicking on a right panel button or by pressing corresponding key + space bar.

Other buttons :
- The eye : Exploded view of the cube (you can't play with the cube while the exploded view is active). - Scramble : Scramble the cube. - Solve : Solve the cube. - Slide bar : Change the speed of the cube rotation.
